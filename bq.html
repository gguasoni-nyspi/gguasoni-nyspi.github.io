The BrainQUANT Institute

Founded by Prof. Boldrini DuPont, the BrainQUANT Institute aims to apply cutting edge quantitative neuroscience methods to understand brain function and diseases pathogenesis. Quantitative cutting-edge technologies are applied to interrogate, at a single cell and regional level, the molecular landscape of brain biology and pathology.
